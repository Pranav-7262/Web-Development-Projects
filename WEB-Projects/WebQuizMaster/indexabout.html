About WebQuizMaster

WebQuizMaster is an interactive quiz platform designed to test and improve users' knowledge of various web development topics, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The platform provides a fun and engaging way to assess and enhance your skills through multiple quizzes with a time-based challenge.

Quiz Categories

  • HTML Quiz: 10 MCQs (15 seconds per question)
  • CSS Quiz: 10 MCQs (15 seconds per question)
  • JavaScript Quiz: 10 MCQs (15 seconds per question)
  • Web Development Quiz: A combined quiz covering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a total duration of 10 minutes

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time Limit: Each question in the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript quizzes must be answered within 15 seconds. The Web Development quiz has a total time limit of 10 minutes.
  • Scoring System: Correct answers earn points, while incorrect or skipped questions do not.
  • No Backtracking: Once a question is answered or time runs out, you cannot go back to change your answer.
  • Single Attempt per Question: Choose your answer wisely! You cannot modify your response once submitted.
